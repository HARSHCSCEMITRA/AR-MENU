FAQ

Common Questions

Do customers need to download an app?
No! ARMenu uses Web AR technology — customers just scan the QR code and it opens in their browser (Chrome on Android, Safari on iPhone). Zero app download, zero friction.
How does AI 3D generation work?
You upload a photo of your dish. Our system sends it to Meshy.ai (primary AI tool) which generates a 3D model in 2-5 minutes. If unavailable, it falls back to Tripo3D or Luma AI automatically.
What phones does AR work on?
Android (Chrome): Works on any Android 8+ device with a decent camera. iPhone (Safari): iOS 14+ with ARKit support. That covers 95%+ of smartphones in India.
Can I upload my own 3D models?
Yes! On Pro and Enterprise plans, you can upload custom .glb or .gltf 3D model files. If you want professional 3D models, we can also recommend 3D artists.
Is there a free trial?
Yes! 14-day free trial on all plans — no credit card required. Full access to all features so you can see real results before paying.
